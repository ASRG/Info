# Pineapple-Tetra

* Dual discrete 2.4/5 GHz a/b/g/n 
* Atheros 2:2 MIMO radios
* 4 onboard Skybridge amplifiers
* 800 mWmax per radio
* Onboard NAND Flash \(2 GB\)
* USB 2.0 Host and RJ45 Ethernet Ports
* Made by [Hak5](Hak5)
